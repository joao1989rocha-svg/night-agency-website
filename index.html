About Us

A corporate approach with nightlife energy

Night Recruitment Agency is a professional model recruitment and management agency with over 12 years of experience across the UK and Europe. We act as a model agent for our talent, helping build professional portfolios and managing negotiations, deals and contracts with companies on their behalf.

Our goal is to recruit talent, develop strong portfolios and connect people with the best opportunities across our client network in modeling, nightlife, hospitality, acting and digital creative work.
